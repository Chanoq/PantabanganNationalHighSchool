SH Strands We Offer

Humanities and Social Sciences Strand (HUMSS)
Science Technology Engineering And Mathematics (STEM)
Accountancy Business Manage (ABM)
Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL)
General Academic Strand (GAS)

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences Strand (HUMSS) The HUMMS strand is designed for
those who wonder what is on the other side of the wall. In other words, you are ready to
take on the world and talk to a lot of people.

STEM

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics strand.
Through the STEM strand, senior high school students are exposed to complex
mathematical and science theories and concepts which will serve as a foundation
for their college courses.

ABM

The Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand—or ABM strand—seeks
to prepare the young business leaders of tomorrow. ABM strand
the way for business-related college degrees. It teaches the basic concepts of financial
management, accounting, and corporate operations.

TVL

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track. Through the TVL senior high school track, students can gain the skills and
they need to seek for employment straight after senior high school whether that's in the
Philippines or overseas. What are the strands to choose from under the TVL track?

GAS

General Academic Strand (GAS). Unlike other K to 12 tracks and strands that are specific to a career,
GAS gives you the chance to weigh your options. GAS is a Senior High School strand that takes on a generalist
approach in preparing students for college.

JH Sections We Offer

Science Technology Engineering (STE)
Information Communication Technology (ICT)
OTHERS

STE

Is a Science, Technology, Engineering, Math and Research oriented curriculum. The program is designed to develop the full
potential of the learners in Science, Technology, Math and Engineering education as well as to repare them for
higher learning and future career in Science, Technology, and Engineering fields.

ICT

ICT encompasses both the internet-enabled sphere as well as the mobile one powered by wireless networks. It also includes antiquated technologies,
such as landline telephones, radio and television broadcast -- all of which are still widely used today alongside
cutting-edge ICT pieces such as artificial intelligence and robotics.

OTHERS

Other section as "Section Sampaguita or Section A or 1". This section is for transferrees from grade 8-10 and for the students
who didn't pass the entrance exam for STE and ICT. This section is also provided with excellent learning procedures.
